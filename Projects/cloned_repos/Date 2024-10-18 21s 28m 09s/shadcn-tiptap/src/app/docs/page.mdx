# Shadcn Tiptap

Sets of custom & extended extensions for tiptap editor.<br />
Easy to use. Customizable. Open Source.  

## Features

- **Custom Extensions**: Easily integrate custom extensions for Tiptap.
- **Shadcn/UI Components**: Compatible with shadcn/ui components.
- **Ready to Use**: Copy & paste for seamless integration.
- **Open Source**: Contribute and adapt it to your needs.
